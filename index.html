"katahum"

12 x 18 in
acrylic on canvas board
2020

"katahum" is a serene abstract painting that embodies tranquility with delicate strokes of lavender and azure hues. At its core is the calm silhouette of a Capizena, her eyes closed in introspection, reflecting the peaceful spirit of this enchanting province. Soft pastel strokes create a soothing atmosphere, while cascading strands of cerulean and pearl symbolize the continuous flow of thoughts and emotions, mirroring the ebb and flow of the Visayan seas. "Capizena" is a visual meditation, inviting contemplation and offering a serene escape into the quiet beauty of its abstract composition, inspired by the tranquil essence of a Capizena maiden.

"void"

16 x 20 in
acrylic on canvas
2022

"Inspired by the Japanese art of Kintsugi, 'Kintsugi Reflections' celebrates the beauty of healing and embracing imperfections. The canvas, adorned with maki-e-inspired strokes, tells a transformative story as fractures are seamlessly mended with gold, silver, and platinum. This piece invites contemplation on the resilience found in accepting flaws. 'Kintsugi Reflections' is a visual journey, where broken fragments become radiant seams, reminding us that healing is an art, and our scars contribute to our unique beauty."

"crab"

12 x 18 in
acrylic on canvas
2022

In the painting "Crab," a lone hand with a watchful eye reaches out for help, symbolizing vulnerability. Yet, a multitude of visionless hands holds it back—an allegory for crab mentality. The eye urges reflection on empathy and shared vision, as "Crab" captures the struggle to break free from collective hindrance and embrace transformative unity.
